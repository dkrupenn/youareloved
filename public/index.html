The story

This project started with a simple idea: what if a small object could carry kindness from one stranger to another — across cities, across countries — one hand at a time?

Each card is laser-engraved on aluminum, made to last. On the front: remember you are loved. On the back, a number and this website. When you find one, you can read its journey here, leave a note, and then — keep it, or pass it on.

Found a card?

Look for the number on the back of the card. Then go to youareloved.art/ followed by that number — for example, a card numbered 0042 would be at youareloved.art/0042. Or just scan the QR code, it goes straight there.

If your card doesn't have a number, reach out and I'll add it.

Want a card?

Cards are made in small batches and left in the world — on park benches, in libraries, coffee shops, anywhere life happens. They're always free. If you'd like to nominate someone who could use one, send me a note.
